Submission Protocol

Mandatory Guidelines for Students

Be Respectful

Use polite and professional language. Avoid abusive or offensive words. Criticism is welcome; disrespect is not.

Honest & Genuine

Share your real experience only. No fake reviews (positive or negative). Avoid exaggeration or misleading info.

Focus on Academics

Discuss teaching quality, clarity, and punctuality. Avoid comments on lifestyle or appearance.

No Personal Attacks

Do not target faculty personally. Keep it constructive, not harmful. No baseless accusations.

Privacy First

Do not share personal details (phone numbers, addresses). Respect privacy of all entities at all times.

Constructive Feedback

Suggest improvements instead of just complaining. (e.g., "Use more real-life examples").

Zero Tolerance

Strictly no hate speech, discrimination (gender, religion, caste), or spam. Stay relevant to academics.

By proceeding, you agree to adhere to these protocols. Submissions violating these rules will be purged.
